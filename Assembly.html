Wireless 8-Lane Scoreboard

Bill of Materials for 8 Lane Wireless Scoreboard

Custom PCA (a total of N+1 Custom PCAs are needed for an N-lane scoreboard)

  • 9x Custom Scoreboard PCB (Eagle CAD files available)
  • 18X 74AHCT245D Octal Transceiver SOIC-20 (Mouser 771-AHCT245D118)
  • 2.1mm x 5.5mm DC barrel jack (Mouser 163-7620E-E)
  • 18X dual row 2x5 0.1" female header (Mouser 571-215309-5)
  • ~250X Breakaway 0.1" Male Headers (Mouser 649-54101-S08-00)
  • 18X 0.1uF 16V 10% X7R 0603 capacitor (Mouser 80-C0603C104K4R7411)

Electronic Parts

  • 9X Moteino Arduino clone with tranceiver and flash
  • 1X USB Moteino with transciever and no flash
  • 68X 16x24 Red LED Matrix Panel Chainable HT1632C Driver (Adafruit part #555)
  • 408X 1206 4RNet 27 Ohm 5% (to increase brightness of LED panels) (Mouser 667-EXB-38V270JV)
  • 84X 2x5 IDC ribbon cable male connectors (need a different part number as I don't like the ones that I last ordered from Mouser)
  • ~50ft Flat Cables 28 AWG Ribbon .050 Zip 10 Cond (Mouser 523-191-2801-110FT)
  • 4X 5V 10A switching power supply (Adafruit part #658)
  • 3X 2-Way 2.1mm DC Barrel Jack Splitter Squid (Adafruit part #1351)
  • Heavy gauge wire for connecting three lanes together (could not find a squid splitter long enough for Event/Heat + 2 lanes)
  • Surge protector
  • Mini-USB to USB cable
  • Small plastic box for USB Moteino

Case/Mechanical Structure

  • 18X 3/4 in. x 1/2 in. x 36 in. Aluminum Flat Angle with 1/16 in. Thick (Home Depot/Lowes/etc)
  • 2X Steelworks 6-ft L x -3/4-in W x -3/4-in H Aluminum Plain Square Tube (Lowes/Home Depot/etc)
  • Red Cast Acrylic (Aero-Shield Plastics in Houston)
  • 1x6 Poplar S4S boards for case (Home Depot/Lowes/etc)
  • 272X 4-40 x 1" female/female hex binding post (Mouser 534-2205)
  • 544x 4-40 3/8" phillips head screws (Mouser 534-9901)
  • 1-1/4 in. Coarse Zinc-Plated Steel Square-Head Pocket Screws (100-Pack) (Home Depot)
  • 1X 1/4 in. 5 ft. x 5ft Baltic Birch Plywood-Grade A (Houston Hardwoods)
  • Paint for wood (less than 1/2 pint is needed to cover wood)
  • Exterior grade urethane for back of plywood
  • Black matte spray paint for interior of plywood and aluminum
